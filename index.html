Carnivore Collection

An IRL NFT Adventure

What is it?

Carnivore Collection is a crossover IRL NFT adventure from the lens of Cindy Goeddel. Cindy is an award winning and world famous wildlife photographer who has spent decades in the wild to obtain these incredible photos. The Carnivore Collection showcases the visceral side of the animal kingdom with 20 Originals unchanged images converted into NFTs and 499 Carnivores which are variations of the Originals for a total collection size of 10k. The NFTs are graphic and may not be suitable for all audiences. Viewer Discretion Advised.

Whats the purpose?

The Carnivore Collection strives to accomplish multiple goals:

(1) Bring awareness and support to the spectacular animals on this planet.

(2) To support, interact with, and learn from an artist who has been at the forefront of her profession for decades.

(3) To create a collection within the existing NFT community that provides an IRL artist (and those who wish to do the same) the ability to build and support communities similar to larger collections.

(4) To join those who have taken the opportunity the NFT community provides to reinvent themselves in the digital world and continue to pursue their passion.

What's the Utility?

The Carnivore Collection will have multiple IRL utilities:

(1) A portion of all mint and royalties will be donated to a wildlife conservation charity.

(2) All Carnivore holders will be granted access to online classes where they can learn photography and lightroom skills directly from Cindy (schedule and frequency to be determined).

(3) All Carnivore holders will be given exclusive access to member only Discord server channels where they can chat with Cindy directly and see new photo drops from Cindys various photography trips around the world.

(4) The 20 Original Carnivore holders will be invited on a trip with Cindy to the exact location, out in the remote bush, wilderness, or forest, where the Original was captured. Oh, and Carnivore Collection will foot the bill for this expedition. Original Carnivore holders merely need to show up ready for the adventure.*

The Collection

The Carnivore Collection is made up of 10,000 unique NFTs. Each NFT is based on a real life Carnivore interaction witnessed and captured by Cindy.

20 Original Carnivores: These are the originals, the inspirations behind the entire collection. They will entitle the holders to special utility. Want to witness a carnivore capture its prey IRL? Get one of these and you just might.

9,980 Carnivores created from the 20 Carnivores: These will have unique traits and represent our take on blending IRL with NFT. Some variations will have additional utility. All Carnivores will give you access to the Carnivore Collection Viewing Platform, thus giving you admission to contests, merch, and other interesting surprises.

Traits

The variations of a particular Carnivore will share some traits with other Carnivore variations but some Carnivores will have traits unique to their Original parent alone. Rarity will be available for those who wish to find it in the form of raw and/or surprising traits. Dont attach yourselves only to numerical rarity, find the Carnivore that represents you, your path, your journey, your passion

Carnivore Color:

Except for the 20 OGs, each NFT will have a distinct Carnivore Color that has been masterfully generated by Aaron the Manipulator.

Prey Color:

Like the carnivore colors, each NFT will also have a distinct Prey Color.

Background

Youll see exotic colors in every background and even the occasional item with special utility.
